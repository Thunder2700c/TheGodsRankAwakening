Year 2058, Earth Venta

It was the years when Scientists started to think about a way to explore parallel Worlds after proving thier exsistance. Wormhole theory, this theory was conceptualized through Einstein's field equations, provides the mathematical foundation for creating traversable bridges between distant point in spacetime.

Dr. Oscar Oyl was the one who was appointed to be Main Researcher of this project and the one who laid basic foundation for this project. That project was named "Project Quantum Worlds" Dr. Oscar belives that Einstein-Rosen bridges represent solutions to general relativity that theoretically allow for shortcuts through the fabric of spacetime.

He also suggested that String theory contributes the mathematical framework for understanding extra dimensions beyond our familiar four-dimensional spacetime. The theory suggests that our universe may contain up to 11 dimensions, with the additional seven dimensions compactified at microscopic scales. Accessing these higher dimensions could provide pathways to parallel universe with fundamentally different properties. It took him 3 years to get permission for starting the project.
