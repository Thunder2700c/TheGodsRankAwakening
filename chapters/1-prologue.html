⚡ The God's Rank Awakening ⚡

PROLOGUE

October 21, 2120, CE

That day was a change in the life of killers.

“No, No, Nooooo, I will not die, I will… kill him, cut down… his head.”
A final battle starts: the Sword and Two-Sided Dragger of the Asura King Kanasura strikes, sending out heavy fire sparks.

KANSASURA says: “This is the right time, put your weapons down and work for me, and I will spare your life.”

Aditya replies: “Work for you? No way in hell. My goal is to take your head, and I am not stopping until I chop it off.”

The sky was crimson red, the land full of ruins and blood, dead bodies of soldiers scattered everywhere.

The killer staggered amid the crimson ruins, shadows flickering at his core like a killer’s awakening system. Killing intent surged, pushing him beyond mortal limits. Blood stained the grounds, but his eyes burned with defiance as KANSASURA circled, spear gleaming with infernal light.

Kansasura said: “I think you will not understand.”

The Asura King snarled, lunging forward with blinding speed, thrusting the spear straight toward the killer’s midsection, poised to pierce between stomach and chest in a fatal strike that could end it all…
