PROLOGUE

October 21, 2120, CE

That day was a change in the life of killers.

“No, No, Nooooo, I will not die, I will... kill him, cut down... his head.”

A final battle starts. The Sword and Two-Sided Dagger of Asura King Kanasura strike, emerging heavy fire sparks.

Kanasura: “This is the right time, put your weapons down and work for me and I will spare your life.”
Aditya: “Work for you? No way in hell. My goal is to take your head, and I will not stop until I chop your head.”

The sky was crimson red, land full of ruins and blood, dead bodies of all soldiers surrounded everywhere.

The killer staggered amid the crimson ruins, shadows flickering at his core like a killer’s awakening system, killing intent surging beyond mortal limits. Blood stained the grounds, but his eyes burned with defiance as Kanasura circled, spear gleaming with infernal light.

Kanasura: “I think you will not understand.”

The Asura King snarled, lunging forward with blinding speed, thrusting the spear straight toward the killer’s midsection, poised to pierce between stomach and chest in a fatal strike that could end it all...
