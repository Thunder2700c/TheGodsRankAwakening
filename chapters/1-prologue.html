Chapter X: The Crimson Awakening

— Part I: The Defiant Will —

The crimson ruins stretched endlessly, mirroring the violent, fractured sky above. **Aditya** stood firm, the obsidian dust settling on his worn combat boots. His core flared with raw power, a stark contrast to the despairing atmosphere.

“You think a mere human can stand against an Asura King?” Kanasura's voice boomed, chilling the air. “You have earned the right to die by my spear.”

Aditya didn't flinch. He raised his gauntlet, which glowed with the familiar, unstable light of his god-rank awakening.

He remembered the words of his mentor: *'A true god is not defined by their rank, but by their will.'*

With a battle cry that shook the shattered skyscrapers, he leaped. The final confrontation had begun.
